EQAITE app

The EQAITE app - Beta coming soon.

The EQAITE app is a web application that allows you to evaluate AI tools against the eqAIte framework  and display the results on a radar map.  It is designed to help you understand the capabilities of AI tools  and make informed decisions about their use.
